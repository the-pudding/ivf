Thank you to my village. Thank you to my husband and family who’ve held me during each failed cycle. Thank you to the nurses, clinical writers, receptionists and the doctors at Generation Next Fertility, the clinic I found after some terrible experiences elsewhere. I didn’t catch the names of every nurse or staffer there, but would like to give a shoutout to Abigail, Kendall, Yamika, Abigail, Jennifer, Sophia, Alex, Elizabeth and Joana. Thank you, Dr. Janelle Luk, for always listening to what I want and for taking on my tricky case, and separately thank you to my OBGYN Dr. Lindsay Amy Goodman, for letting me play Bad Bunny in the lead up to my endometriosis surgery to feel less scared. Thanks to Laura Kradas, my fertility advocate and rock, and to all my friends, Aggi, Giulia, Simone, Atlas, MR, Jon, Tra, Hanna, Rachael, Taylor, Jessie, Sienna, Naina, Naina’s sister, Naina’s mom, Carla, Zahra, and so many more, who sent me care packages, who whisked me away on adventures to distract me, and who firmly believe in my future as a mother, either through birth or through other means.
